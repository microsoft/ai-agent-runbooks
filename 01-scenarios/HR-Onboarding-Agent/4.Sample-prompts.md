# Sample Prompts — HR Onboarding Auto Agent

> These sample prompts are designed to help new hires, HR teams, and CSAs
> get the most out of the **HROnboardingAutoAgent**.  
> Prompts are organized by category based on common onboarding needs.

---

## How to Use These Prompts

- Copy any prompt below and paste it directly into the **HROnboardingAutoAgent** chat
- You can use these prompts in:
  - **Microsoft Teams** — via the agent added under *Built for your org*
  - **M365 Copilot** — via the agent panel on the right navigation
  - **Email** — send the question as the body of an email to the agent's trigger address
- Feel free to modify the prompts to match your specific situation

## Category 1: Company Policies & Compliance

These prompts help new hires quickly understand the rules and expectations of the organization.

| # | Prompt | Expected Output |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | What is the company code of conduct? | Summary of behavioral expectations and ethical guidelines |
| 2 | What are the rules around workplace harassment and discrimination? | Policy details on respectful workplace behavior |
| 3 | What is the policy on remote work and flexible working hours? | Remote work eligibility and scheduling guidelines |
| 4 | What is the travel and expense reimbursement policy? | Step-by-step guidance on submitting expense reports |
| 5 | Are there any policies I need to review before my first day? | List of mandatory policies for new hires |

### Example Interaction

> **User**: What is the company code of conduct?  
> **Agent**: Contoso's Code of Conduct outlines the ethical standards and professional behavior expected of all employees. Key areas include integrity in business dealings, respect for colleagues, protection of company assets, and compliance with applicable laws... *(source: Contoso Employee Handbook — ServiceNow KB)*

## Category 7: Email Trigger Prompts

These prompts are designed to be sent as **email body text** to the agent's trigger email address.
The agent will automatically process the question and reply with an answer.

> 💡 **How to use**: Send an email to the HR Agent trigger address with the prompt below as the email body.
> The agent will reply with relevant information from the ServiceNow knowledge base.

| # | Email Subject (suggested) | Email Body (Prompt) |
|---|---|---|
| 33 | Question about wellness benefits | What wellness benefits are available to new employees? Please provide a detailed overview. |
| 34 | Medical plan inquiry | Can you provide a detailed breakdown of the available medical plans, including providers and coverage? |
| 35 | Onboarding checklist request | What are the key steps I need to complete during my first week of onboarding? |
| 36 | Leave policy question | What types of leave are available and how do I apply for them? |
| 37 | Travel expense question | What is the company travel expense policy and how do I submit a reimbursement request? |
| 38 | Code of conduct question | Please provide a summary of the company code of conduct that I should be aware of as a new hire. |

### Example Email Interaction

> **Email From**: newemployee@contoso.com  
> **Email To**: hragent-trigger@contoso.com  
> **Subject**: Question about wellness benefits  
> **Body**: What wellness benefits are available to new employees? Please provide a detailed overview.
>
> ---
>
> **Auto-reply From**: hragent-trigger@contoso.com  
> **Subject**: Question about wellness benefits  
> **Body**:  
> Thank you for your inquiry. Here is the information I found regarding wellness benefits:
>
> **Health Insurance**: Contoso offers health insurance through Blue Cross Blue Shield.  
> **Dental Insurance**: Dental insurance is provided through Delta Dental.  
> **Vision Coverage**: Vision coverage is available through VSP Vision Care.  
> **Gym Memberships**: Contoso provides memberships to local gyms such as Anytime Fitness and Planet Fitness.  
> **Mental Health Support**: Mental health support services are available through BetterHelp.
>
> *(source: Comprehensive Guide to Health and Wellness Benefits)*

## Tips for Getting Better Responses

| Tip | Example |
|---|---|
| **Be specific** | Instead of *"Tell me about benefits"*, try *"What dental coverage is available and who is the provider?"* |
| **Ask for a specific format** | Add *"display it as a table"* or *"provide a step-by-step list"* to structure the response |
| **Combine questions** | *"What is the medical plan and how do I enroll? Please provide both in detail."* |
| **Ask for contact info** | Add *"and who should I contact for more information?"* to get escalation paths |
| **Follow up** | If the answer is incomplete, ask *"Can you provide more detail on [specific point]?"* |

## Out-of-Scope Topics

The **HROnboardingAutoAgent** is designed to answer questions based on the
configured ServiceNow knowledge base. If a question falls outside the available
knowledge, the agent will respond with:

> *"I don't know, please reach out to HRInfo@[your-tenant].onmicrosoft.com"*

Topics that may be out of scope include:
- Questions about specific individual employee records or personal data
- Real-time IT system status or live support tickets
- Topics not covered in the Contoso Employee Handbook or Wellness Benefits guide
