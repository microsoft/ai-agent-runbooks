# Sample Prompts — Workplace Agent

These sample prompts are designed to help employees and IT administrators get the most out of the **WorkplaceAgent**. Prompts are organized by category based on common internal inquiry and application request scenarios.

---

## How to Use These Prompts

Copy any prompt below and paste it directly into the **WorkplaceAgent** chat.

You can use these prompts in:
- **Microsoft 365 Copilot** — via the agent panel on the right navigation (under *Built for your org*)
- **Microsoft Teams** — via a direct chat with the agent or by @mentioning it in a channel
- **Copilot Studio Test Chat** — for engineer/developer validation and testing

Feel free to modify the prompts to match your specific situation.

> 💡 **Tip:** If you want a structured response (table, list, step-by-step), say so in your prompt — for example: *"Explain in bullet points"* or *"Show me as a table"*.

## Category 1: IT & Systems

These prompts help employees get answers to common IT questions — Microsoft 365 tools, Wi-Fi access, account setup, and system usage.

| # | Prompt | Expected Output |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | What is the internal Wi-Fi SSID and password? | Reference to the IT Environment Setup Guide on the internal portal; note that credentials are not shared verbally for security reasons |
| 2 | What can Copilot in Excel do? | Overview of Excel Copilot capabilities: data analysis, formula creation, chart and PivotTable generation, formatting, and summarization using natural language |
| 3 | Does Microsoft Teams have an automatic interpretation feature? | Explanation of Teams real-time automatic interpretation (AI interpretation / live caption translation), language selection steps, and note on interpreter assignment permissions |
| 4 | How do I reset my password if I am locked out of my account? | Step-by-step password reset instructions via the IT self-service portal and IT helpdesk contact information |
| 5 | How do I set up VPN access on my laptop? | VPN installation and configuration steps, download link for the VPN client, and IT support contact |
| 6 | What software is available for installation on company devices? | List of approved software available via the internal software portal; guidance on requesting additional software |

### Example Interaction

> **User:** What can Copilot in Excel do?
>
> **Agent:** Copilot in Microsoft Excel can significantly enhance your productivity by allowing you to work with data using natural language.
> Here is what you can do:
> - **Data Analysis** — Ask Copilot to analyze trends, highlight outliers, or summarize key insights in your spreadsheet.
> - **Formula Creation** — Describe what you need and Copilot will generate the appropriate formula (e.g., "Calculate the year-over-year growth rate for column B").
> - **Charts & PivotTables** — Prompt Copilot to auto-generate charts or PivotTables without needing to configure them manually.
> - **Formatting** — Ask Copilot to apply conditional formatting or organize data (e.g., "Highlight all cells where sales are below 1,000").
> - **Summarization** — Get a plain-language summary of your data instantly.
>
> No complex functions or macros are required. Simply describe your goal in natural language and Copilot will handle the rest.
>
> *(Source: IT Tools & Microsoft 365 Usage Guide — Internal Knowledge Base)*

## Category 2: HR & Employment Policies

These prompts help employees understand HR regulations, company policies, and employment rules.

| # | Prompt | Expected Output |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 7 | Is a side job (moonlighting) allowed at this company? | Policy summary on side jobs: approval required in principle; guidance to consult HR and obtain approval before starting |
| 8 | What is the remote work policy? | Remote work eligibility criteria, required procedures (e.g., manager approval, IT setup), and any location restrictions |
| 9 | How many days of annual leave am I entitled to? | Annual leave entitlement based on employee tier or tenure, carry-over rules, and how to submit a leave request |
| 10 | What types of leave are available (e.g., sick leave, parental leave, bereavement)? | Full list of available leave types, duration, eligibility criteria, and application procedures for each |
| 11 | What is the performance review cycle and process? | Overview of review periods, self-assessment requirements, manager evaluation steps, and timeline |
| 12 | What are the rules regarding use of company devices for personal use? | Acceptable Use Policy summary for company-issued devices, prohibited activities, and consequences |

### Example Interaction

> **User:** Is a side job allowed at this company?
>
> **Agent:** Side jobs (moonlighting) are governed by Contoso's internal employment regulations.
>
> **In principle, you may not engage in a side job without prior company approval.** The key rules are:
> - If you are considering a side job, you must consult with HR **before** starting.
> - You must obtain formal written approval through the submission process outlined in the employment handbook.
> - Side jobs that conflict with company business interests, require use of confidential information, or negatively impact your primary work performance are **not permitted** regardless of approval.
> - Violations may result in disciplinary action.
>
> **Next steps:** Please contact HR directly at Support@Contoso.com or submit a consultation request via the internal portal.
>
> *(Source: Contoso Employment Regulations — Article 14, Side Job Policy)*

## Category 3: Facilities & Office Operations

These prompts help employees with office-related requests, equipment, and facilities management.

| # | Prompt | Expected Output |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 13 | How can I borrow or purchase office equipment such as a monitor, adapter, or keyboard? | Explanation of the equipment loan/purchase process via the "Procurement" internal portal; General Affairs contact for questions |
| 14 | How do I request building access or an access card? | Process for requesting building access, Security team contact, and expected processing time |
| 15 | Who do I contact if there is a maintenance issue in the office (e.g., a broken chair or projector)? | Facilities management contact information and the procedure for submitting a maintenance request |
| 16 | How do I book a meeting room? | Step-by-step guide for booking meeting rooms via Outlook/Teams calendar and applicable booking rules |
| 17 | Is there a lost and found process for items left in the office? | Lost and found procedure, who to contact, and how long items are kept |

### Example Interaction

> **User:** How can I borrow or purchase office equipment such as a monitor, adapter, or keyboard?
>
> **Agent:** Equipment loan and purchase requests can be submitted through the internal portal **"Procurement"**.
>
> Here is how the process works:
> 1. Log in to the internal portal and navigate to **Procurement → Equipment Request**.
> 2. Select whether you need to **borrow** (temporary use) or **purchase** (permanent assignment) the item.
> 3. Fill in the item details (type, quantity, justification) and submit.
> 4. **If items are in stock:** General Affairs will arrange delivery or collection, typically within 2–3 business days.
> 5. **If purchase is required:** The approval workflow will be initiated automatically. You will receive a notification once your manager has approved the request.
>
> For urgent requests or questions about stock availability, contact General Affairs at **Support@Contoso.com**.
>
> *(Source: Office Operations & Facilities Guide — Internal Knowledge Base)*

## Category 4: Payroll & Personal Information

These prompts help employees manage their payroll details and update personal records.

| # | Prompt | Expected Output |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 18 | How do I update my bank account information for salary payment? | Steps to update bank account via the Employee Self-Service portal, required documentation, and HR contact for verification |
| 19 | How do I update my registered address on file with the company? | Process for submitting an address change via Employee Self-Service, with note on required supporting documents |
| 20 | When is the monthly payroll processing date? | Payroll schedule, first salary payment date for new employees, and notes on public holiday adjustments |
| 21 | How do I submit an expense reimbursement claim? | Step-by-step expense claim submission process, required receipts, submission deadline, and expected reimbursement timeline |
| 22 | Who do I contact if there is a discrepancy in my paycheck? | Payroll team contact information and process for raising a payroll dispute |

### Example Interaction

> **User:** How do I change my registered address and bank account information?
>
> **Agent:** Both your registered address and bank account information can be updated through the **Employee Self-Service** section of the internal portal.
>
> **To update your registered address:**
> 1. Log in to the internal portal → navigate to **Employee Self-Service → Personal Information → Address**.
> 2. Enter your new address and upload a copy of a supporting document (e.g., utility bill or residence certificate) if required.
> 3. Submit the change request — it will be reviewed and approved by HR typically within 3 business days.
>
> **To update your bank account:**
> 1. Navigate to **Employee Self-Service → Personal Information → Bank Account**.
> 2. Enter your new bank account details and upload a copy of your bank statement or passbook for verification.
> 3. Submit the request. Changes take effect from the **next payroll cycle**.
>
> For urgent cases or assistance, please contact HR at **Support@Contoso.com**.
>
> *(Source: Employee Self-Service Guide — HR Internal Portal)*

## Category 5: Application & Request Submission

These prompts **trigger the application workflow** — the agent will guide you through submitting a formal request that is routed for manager approval.

| # | Prompt | Expected Output |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 23 | I would like to submit an equipment loan request | Agent guides user through equipment loan application form fields (item type, duration, justification), submits to Dataverse, and triggers the approval flow |
| 24 | I would like to apply for remote work for next week | Agent guides user through remote work application (dates, work location, manager), submits request, and initiates approval notification |
| 25 | I want to submit a leave application for paid time off | Agent guides user through leave application (leave type, dates, reason), confirms details, and triggers the approval and notification flow |
| 26 | I would like to request approval for a business trip | Agent collects trip details (destination, dates, purpose, estimated cost), confirms, and routes for manager approval |
| 27 | I need to submit an expense reimbursement request | Agent guides user through expense claim form (date, amount, category, receipt attachment instructions), submits to Dataverse, and triggers approval flow |

### Example Interaction

> **User:** I would like to submit an equipment loan request.
>
> **Agent:** Sure! I will help you submit an equipment loan request. Please answer the following questions:
>
> 1. **What type of equipment do you need?** (e.g., laptop, monitor, keyboard, adapter, webcam)
>
> **User:** A portable monitor.
>
> **Agent:** Got it — a portable monitor.
> 2. **How long do you need to borrow it?** (e.g., 1 week, 1 month, indefinitely)
>
> **User:** About 2 weeks, starting from Monday.
>
> **Agent:** Understood.
> 3. **Please briefly describe the reason for the request.** (e.g., working from home, attending an off-site event)
>
> **User:** I will be working from home next week and need a second screen.
>
> **Agent:** Thank you. Here is your request summary:
> - **Item:** Portable monitor
> - **Loan period:** 2 weeks starting Monday, March 31, 2026
> - **Reason:** Remote work / home office setup
>
> Shall I submit this request for approval? (Yes / No)
>
> **User:** Yes.
>
> **Agent:** Your equipment loan request has been submitted successfully. 🎉
> - **Request ID:** REQ-2026-0421
> - Your manager has been notified and will review the request. You will receive a notification in Teams once a decision is made.
>
> *(Application record created in Dataverse — Approval flow triggered)*

## Tips for Getting Better Responses

| Tip | Example |
|-----|---------|
| **Be specific** | Instead of *"Tell me about equipment"*, try *"How do I borrow a portable monitor for 2 weeks?"* |
| **Ask for a specific format** | Add *"show as a table"*, *"provide as bullet points"*, or *"give me step-by-step instructions"* to structure the response |
| **Combine questions** | *"What is the remote work policy and how do I submit a remote work application? Please provide both."* |
| **Ask for contact info** | Add *"and who should I contact for more information?"* to get escalation contact details |
| **Follow up** | If the answer is incomplete, ask *"Can you provide more detail on [specific point]?"* or *"What is the next step after that?"* |

## Out-of-Scope Topics

The **WorkplaceAgent** is designed to answer questions based on the configured SharePoint knowledge base and handle applications via the configured Power Automate workflows.

If a question falls outside the available knowledge or workflow scope, the agent will respond with:

> *"I'm sorry, I don't have information on that topic in my knowledge base. Please reach out to the relevant department directly, or contact the helpdesk at Support@Contoso.com."*

**Topics that may be out of scope include:**

- Questions about specific individual employee records, salary details, or confidential personnel data
- Real-time IT system status, live outage reports, or active security incidents
- External customer-facing inquiries or partner/vendor matters
- Topics not covered in the FAQ list or document library configured for this agent
- Requests requiring legal advice or formal HR investigation procedures
- Complex multi-step approvals that fall outside the agent's configured workflow scope

> ⚠️ **For CSAs & Administrators:** If users consistently ask out-of-scope questions, consider adding those topics to the SharePoint FAQ list or expanding the agent's knowledge sources. Use the **FAQ Recording Flow** to capture unresolved inquiry tickets as candidates for FAQ expansion.
