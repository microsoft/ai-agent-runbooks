## Scenario A — Insurance: Policy Q&A Agent

**Context:**
An underwriter needs to verify policy coverage details quickly and accurately.
Manual lookup across hundreds of policy PDFs is slow and error-prone.

> An underwriter types: *"Does our commercial property policy cover flood damage in Zone AE?"*

**Agent Flow:**

1. Agent receives query → Foundry IQ plans search strategy
2. Searches `policy-documents` index (SharePoint PDF store) using vector + keyword hybrid
3. Retrieves matching policy clause with section reference
4. GPT-4o synthesizes answer with exact clause number cited
5. Underwriter gets a precise, auditable answer in < 5 seconds

**Applied Tier:** 🔵 **Tier 2 — AI Search Backed**

> Policy documents are stored across multiple SharePoint sites, often include scanned legacy PDFs,
> and require citation at the clause level. Tier 2's hybrid search and OCR support make it
> the right fit. Tier 3 would apply if live CRM or claims data were needed alongside policy documents.

**Result:** 70% reduction in policy look-up calls to the legal team.

## Scenario B — Healthcare: Clinical Protocol Retrieval

**Context:**
Clinical staff need immediate access to treatment protocols. Protocols are access-controlled
by ward and must be cross-referenced with the drug formulary for safe, complete guidance.

> A nurse asks: *"What is the ICU sedation protocol for patients on CRRT?"*

**Agent Flow:**

1. Agent queries `clinical-protocols` index (curated PDF library, permission-trimmed by ward)
2. Foundry IQ cross-references `drug-formulary` Dataverse table
3. Response includes protocol steps + formulary dosing, with document links
4. Nurse sees cited source — can verify before acting

**Applied Tier:** 🟣 **Tier 3 — Foundry IQ**

> This scenario requires simultaneous retrieval from **two different source types**:
> an unstructured document index (clinical protocols in SharePoint) and a structured
> Dataverse table (drug formulary). Only Tier 3's multi-source agentic orchestration
> can combine these in a single grounded response.

**Result:** Reduced protocol look-up time from 8 minutes (manual) to under 30 seconds.

## Scenario C — HR: Benefits & Policy Self-Service

**Context:**
Employees frequently ask HR the same policy questions — parental leave entitlements,
regional benefits, effective dates. HR teams spend significant time answering repetitive queries.

> An employee asks: *"How many days of parental leave am I entitled to in India?"*

**Agent Flow:**

1. Agent searches `hr-global-policies` SharePoint site, filtered by `region:India`
2. Returns the specific HR circular with effective date
3. If policy has changed recently, the indexer's 30-minute refresh ensures current content

**Applied Tier:** 🔵 **Tier 2 — AI Search Backed**

> The HR policy library spans multiple SharePoint sites with regional metadata filters
> (`region`, `effective_date`, `country`). Tier 2's custom metadata indexing and
> cross-site search capability make this precise filtering possible at scale.
> Tier 1 would work for a single-site deployment with fewer than 1,000 documents.

**Result:** HR ticket volume for common policy questions drops by 45%.

## Scenario D — Legal: Contract Clause Search

**Context:**
A legal team needs to audit contracts for specific clause types across a large archive,
including pre-2015 documents that exist only as scanned PDFs.

> A lawyer asks: *"Find all NDAs with a non-compete clause longer than 24 months."*

**Agent Flow:**

1. Agent uses AI Search with a **structured filter** on `doc_type:NDA` + semantic search on `"non-compete duration"`
2. Returns ranked results with clause excerpts and document links
3. Lawyer reviews 12 contracts in 3 minutes instead of 3 hours

**Applied Tier:** 🔵 **Tier 2 — AI Search Backed**

> The contract archive includes scanned legacy PDFs requiring OCR, and the search requires
> metadata-level filtering (`doc_type`) combined with semantic retrieval.
> These capabilities are exclusive to Tier 2 and above.
> Tier 3 would be appropriate if contract data needed to be joined with live matter
> management systems or financial records.

**Result:** Contract review time reduced by 90% for standard clause searches.
